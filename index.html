Fun Facts

  • The word "constellation" is derived from the Latin term, meaning "set of stars".
  • The largest constellation is Hydra, taking up 3.16% of the sky!
  • There are 88 officially recognized constellations.
  • The big and little dipers are not actually constellations, they're asterisms!
